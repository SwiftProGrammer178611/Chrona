# Chrona 
## Track your coding time, grow your forest!
You can use Chrona to track your coding time when connected to Hackatime. From there, you can go in your forest and see your forest grow live, as you code and the minutes stack on!
Try it out!: https://chrona-five-hazel.vercel.app/

# Instructions:
- Enter website, click get started
- in dashboard, see shop, time tracked and directions to your forest
- in forest view, there are different tiers to explore and unlock based on your coding progress, with the highest being cherry trees. Your background forest can also grow as time goes on as well
